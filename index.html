Rule:

  • The game is played between two players.
  • Each player takes turns rolling a dice.
  • If the player rolls a 1, their turn ends and they lose all the points accumulated in that turn.
  • If the player rolls any other number, that number is added to their current score.
  • The player can choose to hold their current score at any point and add it to their total score.
  • The first player to reach a total score of 100 wins the game.
